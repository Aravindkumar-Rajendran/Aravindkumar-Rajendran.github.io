We may heard of Artificial Intelligence shortly **AI** many times whenever watching an interview of a tech company CEO saying "We are using Artificial Intelligence in our products" or in a new product launch "an electric bike powered by AI". AI has become the marketing terminology of the modern world and it sometimes emphasized more than any other things. But you know, the real world applications of AI is much more than we can think. ## **What?**

What is Artificial Intelligence?

<br><br>

We have seen in many science fiction movies and in comics. AI is not what we can see only in science fiction movies like Terminator, Chappie, iRobot and etc. Those are mere dramatization if you consider the current state of AI. When you think of AI, do not connect that with robots. As a matter of fact, robot is nothing more than a shell to power the technology. That means, AI can be made without a physical body like limbs and actuators. It is much like a software program which lives and runs in silicon. 

Knowingly or unknowingly we are using AI in our everyday life. Whether it be getting a video or movie recommendations in Youtube and Netflix, or friend suggestions in Facebook or having a talk with Alexa and Siri or sitting in the cars that drive by themselves are using AI to facilitate the feature. AI makes the systems to run and act intelligently.

**Popular techniques to implement AI:**

1. Machine learning 
2. Deep learning
3. Reinforcement learning

- **Machine learning** is the approach that provides systems the ability to automatically learn and improve from data without being explicitly programmed.

<br>

- **Deep learning** is a sub-set of machine learning that deals with biological inspired algorithm known as artificial neural network for automatic feature extraction and black box learning.

<br>

- **Reinforcement learning** is more like a trial and error method of learning from experience where an agent acts in a environment gets rewarded if doing good and gets penalized if doing not good.

## **Why?**

Why we need Artificial Intelligence?
<br><br>

John McCarthy, an American computer scientist, in 1956 at The Dartmouth Conference coined the word Artificial Intelligence with the vision for creating intelligence in the computer systems.

With that envisioned, AI seems to be the most promising than any other technology. I am saying this because we know that we humans are the superior beings in the Earth due to our Intelligence. Our thinking capabilities and our adaptability to change for the environment around us makes our way to top in the food chain. What if we create that intelligence in the computer systems artificially? That can work all the time without sleep and scales like anything (intelligence of 7.2 Billion humans in a small data center). That would be incredible and our potential to discover new things will surge multiple folds and it may recreate our understandings and beliefs about the universe.

But this is called as AGI - Artificial General Intelligence or Human level Intelligence which does not exists till now. Still there are lot of advancements required to achieve that. And whatever we have seen as the examples of AI in the above section are shallow AI. They are just good at specifies task and can not do all the tasks which a human can do. A long way to go.
<br><br><br><br>

### **Advanced AI - envisioning for AGI:**

<br>

**IBM Watson** - AI based information retrieval system which beats humans in Jeopardy game.

<br>

**AlphaGo** - Reinforcement learning based AI which beats humans in an ancient and complex game called Go.

<br>

**AlphaStar** - Reinforcement learning based AI which beats team of humans in a strategy video game called Starcraft.

<br>

**AlphaZero** - Successor of AlphaGo and AphaStar which can beat humans in multiple games like Go, Chess, and other video games..

**AlphaFold** - One of the most advanced AI in the medical domain which made great achievement in analyzing the protein folding.

<br>

**OpenAI Five** - Reinforcement learning based AI which beats team of humans in a strategy video game called Dota 2.

<br>

**GPT3** - Generative Pre-trained Transformer 3 is a transformers based deep contextual word embedding that can produce human-like text.
